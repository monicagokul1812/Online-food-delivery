# Online-food-delivery
Analysis of online food delivery data aimed at uncovering actionable business insights. Using real-world datasets, the project explores customer behavior, order patterns, delivery efficiency, and revenue trends. It leverages data cleaning, EDA statistical techniques, and visualizations to identify key drivers of business performance.
*Analysis of order frequency, delivery times, and distance impact
*Insights into restaurant performance and cuisine popularity
*Revenue, discount, and pricing trend analysis
